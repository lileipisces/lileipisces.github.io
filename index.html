Biography

I am a post-doc supervised by Prof. Li Chen at the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. I visited Rutgers University and did research with Dr. Yongfeng Zhang in 2023. Under their guidance and with the support from Hong Kong Research Grants Council (RGC), I have been investigating large language models for recommender systems. I did my Ph.D. at the same department, and my research focus was mainly on explainable recommendation (particularly in the form of natural language).

Previously, I received my bachelor's degrees in computer science and mathematics from a special program of the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, where I systematically studied recommender systems and information retrieval, under the supervision of Prof. Weike Pan.
