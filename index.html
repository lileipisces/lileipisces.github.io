Biography

I am a year-4 Ph.D. student supervised by Dr. Li Chen at the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. Besides my supervisor, I am so fortunate to have the opportunity of discussing research problems with Dr. Yongfeng Zhang and Dr. Ruihai Dong. Under their guidance, I have been studying the topic of explainable recommendation (or more broadly explainable artificial intelligence).

Previously, I received my double bachelor's degrees in computer science and mathematics with the outstanding graduate honor from a special program of the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, where I systematically studied recommender systems and information retrieval, under the supervision of Dr. Weike Pan.
