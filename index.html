Hi. I'm Lei Li (李磊).

I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Dr. Li Chen at the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. Besides my supervisor, I am so fortunate to have advice from Dr. Yongfeng Zhang and Dr. Ruihai Dong. Under their guidance, I have been working on explainable recommendation.

Previously, I received my double bachelor's degree in computer science and mathematics with the outstanding graduate honor from a special program of the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, where Dr. Weike Pan introduced me to the amazing world of recommender systems and information retrieval.
